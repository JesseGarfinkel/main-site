Who is Jesse Garfinkel?

Jesse is a high school economics teacher in Chicago, a resident of Lakeview, and candidate for State Representative in Illinois's 12th District.

Why is he running for state rep?

Jesse’s students used to enjoy after-school programs. The budget crisis in Springfield cut fudning for those programs. This isn’t something Jesse can stand for. He’s running for State Representitive because Springfield is messing with our kids’ futures.

What is Jesse’s platform?

The heart of Jesse’s platform is funding our schools. To do that, Jesse thinks we’ll need to change our politics. Get special-interest money out of Illinois politics. And challenge the Democratic Machine in Springfield.

Jesse is gathering ideas from Chicagoans for how we could make our state work.
