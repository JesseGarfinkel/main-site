Who is Jesse Garfinkel?

Jesse is a high school economics teacher in Chicago, a resident of Lakeview, and candidate for State Representative in Illinois's 12th District.

He is committed to getting special interest money out of our politics, fixing our state's fiscal problems, and fighting for our schools.

Why is Jesse running for state rep?

Jesse’s students used to have access to after-school programs and vital social services. Then the state budget disaster ended funding for those programs.

Illinois' political stagnation and its devastating effects on our children drove Jesse to take action. We need to change our politics because our current leaders are too beholden to special interests to act in our best interest. Progress can't wait any longer.

What is Jesse’s platform?

Jesse is an advocate for progressive issues and good government.

Jesse is fighting to take special-interest money out of Springfield. He's starting with his own campaign, which won't accept any corporate donations.
