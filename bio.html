About Jesse

Jesse is currently a high school social studies teacher. He has taught every grade from 7th through 12th and currently teaches U.S. History and Civics. He has coached boys and girls soccer and advises numerous clubs. Jesse earned a B.A. in History and Political Science and an M.S. in Education, both from Northwestern University.

Jesse is active in his community, serving on the economic justice team of ONE Northside, an advocacy group that works to eliminate injustice through community organizing. He is the treasurer of his condo association and is a member of Anshe Emet Synagogue.

Jesse is married to the love of his life, Maggie, who is a school counselor. Jesse and Maggie welcomed a baby girl, Sadie, in April. They live in Lakeview with their dog, Simon.
